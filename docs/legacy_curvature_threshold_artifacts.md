# BHSM Legacy Curvature-Threshold Artifacts

Status: author-supplied legacy theory corpus, indexed offline.

The v1.1 audit bundles and hashes four documents by Norman P. Carberry:

- *Mass from Local Curvature Thresholds in a Scalar Topographic Effective Field Theory*;
- *A Curvature-Threshold Scalar Topographic EFT as a Mass-Gap Analogue of Yang-Mills Theory*;
- *Mass Without Higgs: A Geometric Framework for Emergent Particle Mass and Hyperspherical Gravity*;
- *The Origin of Mass*.

The corpus supports the curvature operator `K[rho] = -nabla^2 ln rho`, the
local quantity `k_loc = K[rho](r_c)`, the single-activation convention
`N_K = 1`, and the mass functional `m = (c^2/(2G)) r_c^2 k_loc`.

The principal EFT paper explicitly describes the mass functional as a
geometric matching ansatz rather than a derivation from its scalar action. The
repository preserves that qualification. The mass-gap manuscript is scalar-EFT
spectral support only; it is not a Yang-Mills proof.

Legacy particle threshold tables are treated as illustrative calibration examples, not no-fit BHSM derivations.
