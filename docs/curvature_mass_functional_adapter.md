# Curvature Mass Functional Adapter

The adapter records

```text
m = (c^2/(2G)) r_c^2 k_loc
E = (c^4/(2G)) r_c^2 k_loc
```

with status `ARTIFACT_BACKED_CURVATURE_MASS_FUNCTIONAL`. This status means the
formula is present in a bundled author-supplied theory artifact. It does not
mean the formula is action-derived, experimentally validated, or already
identified with the BHSM neutral boundary response.

The legacy curvature-threshold mass functional supplies a candidate mass bridge, not an empirical neutrino mass prediction by itself.

The scalar mass-gap paper separately records

```text
L = 1/2(dt phi)^2 - 1/2(grad phi)^2
    - lambda/2(-nabla^2 phi - k_loc)^2
m_gap = sqrt(lambda) k_loc
```

as action/spectral support for a curvature-threshold mechanism.
