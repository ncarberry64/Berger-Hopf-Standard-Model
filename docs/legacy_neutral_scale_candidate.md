# Legacy Neutral Scale Candidate

Overall status: `OPEN_MISSING_PROPAGATION_LOCALIZATION_RADIUS`.

The candidate equation is

```text
m_nu,BHSM = (c^2/(2G)) r_prop^2 k_neutral,eff.
```

The functional form is artifact-backed in the legacy theory corpus. BHSM also
has a dimensionless neutral threshold response. The sprint found neither a
physical `r_prop` nor a conversion to `k_neutral,eff` in `m^-2`, so no eV/GeV
mass is emitted.

The electron-neutrino upper limit, PDG values, and W mass are not used as theorem inputs.
