# BHSM Neutral Boundary Measure

The controlling author ontology places the neutral propagation action under
the symbolic measure `dmu_boundary dt`. This establishes where a measure
belongs, but not its physical dimension or normalization.

Status: `OPEN_MISSING_BOUNDARY_MEASURE`.

The measure is linked to the neutral action scaffold. A physical unit theorem
still requires its normalized dimension, the background stiffness or energy
density, and their coupling to the neutral curvature response.
