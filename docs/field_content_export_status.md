# BHSM Field-Content Export Status v0.2

The field-content export is a sector-level source ledger. It may identify BHSM
sectors such as charged leptons, up quarks, down quarks, neutral sector, CKM,
PMNS, and CP holonomy sources.

It does not provide a complete Standard Model particle table, PDG IDs, physical
spin assignments, mass parameters, width parameters, or complete gauge
representations.
