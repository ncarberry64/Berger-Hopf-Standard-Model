# BHSM Neutral Propagation Radius

Status: `CONDITIONAL_PROPAGATION_RADIUS_CANDIDATE`.

The author ontology defines `r_prop` as the characteristic support scale of a
propagating physical neutral boundary field. Its domain, length dimension,
role in the curvature bridge, and zero-propagation compatibility are explicit.
No local artifact supplies a numerical value in metres.

The audit keeps these alternatives separate:

- legacy `r_c`: a general localization variable, not neutrino-specific;
- `sigma`, overlap width, and internal profile radius: dimensionless proxies;
- empirical de Broglie, W-scale, mass-derived, and legacy-table radii: forbidden
  theorem inputs.

A symbolic propagation radius does not by itself produce a physical neutrino mass.
