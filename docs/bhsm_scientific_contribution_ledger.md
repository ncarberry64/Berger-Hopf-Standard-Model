# BHSM scientific contribution ledger

## How to read “achievement” here

This ledger records pull requests that materially changed the explanatory
content of the BHSM research program. An achievement may be:

- a positive mathematical derivation;
- a conditional physical mechanism with named dependencies;
- an obstruction or kill test that rules out an attractive shortcut;
- a reproducibility result that makes a claim independently checkable.

“New” means new within the documented BHSM construction. It does not assert
priority over the scientific literature, independent confirmation, or a new
experimental discovery. Pull-request tests show that the repository behaves
as specified; they do not by themselves validate BHSM Physics.
