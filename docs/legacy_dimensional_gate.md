# Legacy Dimensional Gate

The legacy gravitational curvature expression is dimensionally gated because K has units L^-2 and (c^2/G) r^2 K has units M/L, not M.

BHSM does not use the legacy gravitational curvature expression as a direct particle mass formula.

The expression is retained for provenance as a
`DIMENSIONALLY_GATED_LEGACY_FUNCTIONAL` and
`LEGACY_GRAVITATIONAL_STIFFNESS_ANSATZ`. It may describe a stiffness-like or
mass-per-length bridge. It cannot emit kg, eV, or GeV particle mass unless a
further action-derived length or measure normalization is supplied.
