# Neutral Admissible Domain

The controlling author clarification defines a conditional
measurement-supported neutral response cone. Its coordinates are nonnegative
magnitudes of supported channel responses, not raw signed or complex
wave-amplitude coordinates:

```text
x_i >= 0,
sum_i x_i^2 = 1,
propagation_response > 0,
interaction, detector-boundary, or measurement support active.
```

BHSM treats the neutrino mass contribution as interaction-supported and propagation-locked. Measurement supplies a physical boundary/interaction condition that can prevent the neutral response from nulling out.

This does not say that a neutrino ceases to exist without an observer. The
domain identification is author-ontology conditional and remains to be derived
from the complete neutral action.
