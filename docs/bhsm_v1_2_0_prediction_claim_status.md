# BHSM v1.2.0 prediction claim status

## Allowed

- BHSM v1.2.0 includes an offline Python computational interface.
- BHSM v1.2.0 includes a prediction registry and CLI.
- The registry separates anchors, conditional predictions, upper-limit
  comparisons, frozen internal artifacts, theorem blockers, and software gates.
- W may be used as a calibration anchor; then it is not an independent prediction.
- Electron-neutrino comparison is upper-limit based by default.

## Forbidden

- Claiming this release empirically validates BHSM.
- Claiming W is independently predicted when used as the calibration anchor.
- Claiming a central electron-neutrino mass measurement or validation.
- Claiming validated FeynRules/UFO/MadGraph or official CERN integration.
- Claiming export of the complete BHSM 4D Lagrangian.
- Promoting blockers or runtime-disabled gates to production readiness.
