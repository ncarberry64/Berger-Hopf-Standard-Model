## Statement

For a future FeynRules/UFO interface, vector and fermion fields are represented
in canonical kinetic normalization. Therefore, in the production field basis:

```text
Z_A,prod = 1
Z_psi,prod = 1
```

This clears the interface normalization gate only. It is a basis definition,
not an empirical fit and not a nontrivial BHSM dynamical prediction.

BHSM geometric information enters through projected coupling matrices, mixing
matrices, holonomy phases, mass-width schemes, and production coupling maps,
not through noncanonical kinetic prefactors in the production UFO field basis.
