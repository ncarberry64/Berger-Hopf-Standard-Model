# BHSM Neutrino Curvature Threshold

The artifact-backed neutral constants are

```text
g_nu = 1/3
kappa_nu = 1/6
```

For a normalized neutral boundary state, the conditional response law is

```text
R_coupled = p g_nu ||K_nu psi||/||psi||
R_excess = max(0, R_coupled - kappa_nu)
```

“Push back against the background” means that this dimensionless coupled
kernel response exceeds `kappa_nu`. The constants are artifact-backed; the
interpretation of their positive excess as propagation mass is conditional on
the author ontology.
