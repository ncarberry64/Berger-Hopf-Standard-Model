# Axioms and Admitted Inputs

The implemented framework admits:

- The Standard Model chiral representation pattern.
- A Higgs-selected U(1) normalization with `Y_H = 1/2`.
- Yukawa invariance for charged fermions.
- Anomaly cancellation as an audit condition.
- The supplied Berger scalar spectrum proxy and mode ledger.
- The topographic operator form as a computable spectral problem.
