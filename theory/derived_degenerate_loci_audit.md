# Degenerate Loci Audit

The explicit symbolic minor route can fail on proper degenerate loci, including:

- axis collapse such as \(\beta_0=0\) without a derived finite-width rescue term;
- vanishing universal finite-width second moments;
- noninjective local Wigner/Hopf jets at \(y_0\);
- coincident reduced-Wigner beta factors;
- moment contractions projecting all generation columns into a lower-dimensional subspace.

The branch reports these hazards rather than hiding them.
