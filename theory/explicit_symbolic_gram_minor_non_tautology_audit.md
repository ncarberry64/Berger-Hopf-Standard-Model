# Explicit Symbolic Gram/Minor Non-Tautology Audit

The branch avoids the tautology of declaring a determinant nonzero by assigning unrelated symbols to every entry.

Guardrails:

- entries are Wigner/Hopf jet expressions tied to \((k,\ell,n,m)\);
- derivative rows are local jets of those same expressions;
- finite-width moments are universal, not generation-fitted;
- \(\beta_0\) is not chosen from masses or mixing;
- no CKM, PMNS, or numerical Yukawa data are imported.

Conclusion: the construction is a valid explicit symbolic proof target, but the nonzero determinant theorem remains open.
