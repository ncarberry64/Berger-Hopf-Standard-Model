# Conditional Theorem

Conditional on the admitted chiral representation pattern, Higgs-selected
U(1), supplied mode ledger, and topographic operator assumptions, the framework
is tested for:

- Standard Model hypercharge consistency.
- One-generation anomaly cancellation.
- Charged-sector hierarchy screens.
- Electroweak coupling matching screens.
- Electroweak scale screening.
- A finite proxy spectral-gap bound for `H_T`.

The full no-extra-light-state theorem remains open until the actual spectrum
of `H_T` on the relevant Hilbert space is computed.
