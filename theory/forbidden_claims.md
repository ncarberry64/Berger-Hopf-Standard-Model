# Forbidden Claims

This project must not claim:

- A rigorous first-principles derivation of the Standard Model from pure
  geometry alone.
- A proof of Yang-Mills confinement.
- Completion of the no-extra-light-state theorem before the spectrum of
  `H_T` is computed.
- Neutrino masses as part of the minimal Standard Model.
- Numerical matches as predictions before mode-selection rules are derived
  independently.
