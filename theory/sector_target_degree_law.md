# Sector Target Degree Law

Status: `SECTOR_TARGET_DEGREE_LIFT_LAW_STRUCTURAL_CANDIDATE`

Candidate target-degree rule:

```text
Omega_star = 3 * 2 ** (3B + (3B)(1/2 - T3))
```

The proposed interpretation is that `3` is the primitive charged/lepton
coherent boundary cover, the up-quark channel receives a colored lift to `6`,
and the down-quark channel receives a colored lower-doublet lift to `12`.

| Sector | B | L | T3 | O_q | O_j | |Omega_star| |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| charged lepton | 0 | 1 | -1/2 | -1 | +2 | 3 |
| neutrino | 0 | 1 | +1/2 | -1 | -2 | 3 |
| up | 1/3 | 0 | +1/2 | +1 | -2 | 6 |
| down | 1/3 | 0 | -1/2 | +1 | +4 | 12 |

Neutrinos use the same target magnitude as charged leptons but the sign is
supplied by:

```text
Omega_nu = -q - 2j = -k
```

This note records the law as a structural candidate only. It must not be used to update frozen predictions.
