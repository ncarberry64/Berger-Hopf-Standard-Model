# BHSM v2.0 Roadmap Note

The BHSM completion campaign builds an integrated dependency graph and theorem
status ledger. It separates frozen model outputs from action-derived,
boundary-functional, parent-action, finite-basis, semi-analytic, candidate, and
open claims.

Highest-priority open obligations:

1. prove the full twisted Dirac / `(H_T)` spectrum and infinite-basis
   complement bound;
2. prove `Index(D_twist)=3` and complete mirror-mode exclusion from the full
   operator;
3. prove scalar/topographic decoupling from the full internal action;
4. complete precision QCD/RG threshold matching for quark mass comparisons;
5. derive full flavor matrices from the complete action rather than
   internal-rule screens.

The integrated graph reports no hidden circularity and no dependency on
empirical residual machinery. It does not claim a completed first-principles
derivation of the Standard Model.
