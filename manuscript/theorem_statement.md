# Theorem Statement Draft

Conditional on the admitted Standard Model chiral representation pattern,
Higgs-selected U(1), supplied Berger-Hopf mode ledger, and specified
topographic stability operator, the framework reproduces the anomaly-free
hypercharge ledger and supplies numerical screens for charged-sector
hierarchies, electroweak coupling relations, and the electroweak scale.

The no-extra-light-state statement is not complete until the spectrum of
`H_T` restricted to `H_perp` is computed and shown to obey the stated bound.
